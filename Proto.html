Smart-ordering Table

Description of the product

This smart-ordering table serves as an all-in-one product for the customer. They can call the waiter, order food/drink items, and even pay their tab, without leaving the table. The table offers tap-to-pay, credit card insert or slide, and a cash/coin slot. The purpose of this table is to offer customers a modernized way to dine and have autonomy throughout their experience.
